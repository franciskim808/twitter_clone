Main Social Network Features


Sign Up

Sign up easily with a username, email and password. You can sign in directly if already have an account.

Sign In

Sign in with a username and password and remember account details if needed.

Edit Profile

Edit username and email on your profile. Also changes your username and email for sign in.

Posting

Make a post and share your thoughts with other users!

Search posts

Need to search for a post? Try our search bar :D

Follow and Unfollow

Follow other users to keep up with them! You can see just their posts on the feed. You can unfollow other users too. Don't hurt their feelings ;)

Messaging

Message another user in the messaging section and wait for their reply...

Sign Out

All done? Sign out with just a single click!

Like posts (In progress)

Don't be shy to show love to posts you like :)

Forgot your password? Reset with an email to you (In progress)
